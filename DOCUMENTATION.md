## üë§ 2. User Roles & Workflows

### üß≥ A. The Traveller (Guest)
The person looking for a stay.
- **Journey**: 
    1. Lands on the cinematic Home Page.
    2. Searches for a destination (e.g., Jaipur, Mumbai).
    3. Browses high-quality hotel cards with luxury previews.

    6. Views all past and upcoming bookings in their **Guest Dashboard**.

### üè® B. The Hotel Owner (Partner)
The person who owns and manages the property.
- **How to access**: Register as an 'Owner' or change your role in profile.
- **Owner Dashboard**:
    - **Total Revenue**: See exactly how much money has been earned.
    - **Manage Hotels**: Add new properties or edit existing ones.
    - **Image Upload**: Drag-and-drop up to 6 luxury photos per hotel (powered by Cloudinary).
    - **Amenity Management**: Tag hotels with WiFi, Pool, Gym, etc., using a simple icon-based interface.

    - **Platform Health**: View total site-wide revenue and total booking counts across all hotels.

## üõ†Ô∏è 3. Step-by-Step Instructions

### How to add a new Hotel (Owner Profile):
1. Log in to your account.
2. Go to the **Owner Dashboard**.
3. Click the **+ Add Hotel** button.
4. **Step 1 (Info)**: Enter the Hotel Name and Description.
5. **Step 2 (Location)**: Select an Indian City and State. Set your Price per Night.
6. **Step 3 (Photos)**: Drag and drop your property images. Wait for the green checkmark (Cloudinary upload).
7. **Step 4 (Amenities)**: Click the icons (WiFi, AC, etc.) to highlight what you offer.
8. Click **Submit**. Your hotel is now sent to the Admin for approval!

### How to book a Room (Guest Profile):
1. Browse the hotels on the Home or Search Page.
2. Click **View Details** on a hotel you like.
3. Select an available Room Category.
4. Review the final price (inclusive of taxes/fees).
5. Click **Pay Securely**.
6. Complete the Razorpay transaction. Your booking is instantly confirmed!
